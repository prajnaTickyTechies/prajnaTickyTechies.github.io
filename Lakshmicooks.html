SOTHI SAPADU. more commonly reffered as sothi.Sothi is a Traditional dish originated from tirunelveli,tamilnadu,india and its very healthy as it contains multiple vegetables.it's a very heavy meal and a perfect lunch.It pairs very well with a ginger thoyagal/chutney and some fried potatoes.sothi is also reffered to as "Maapillai sothi" as it is served as maincourse for newly weds.its a rich and scrumptious meal combo.sothi is made up of coconut,milk,veggies,dal and more will please your tastesbuds for sure.Sothi is a treasure of Tirunelveli's vegetarian cuisne.Even hotels in tirunelveli serves this lunch combo on special days.Also ,the balance in this lunch combo draws attention since it is a heavy meal to make it easily digestible.It is so combined with ginger thoyagal/chutney that when they both combine it helps in easy digestion.In conclusion SOTHI is a wonderful dish that words cannot describe just click the button to see a video on how to make this delightful dish.
